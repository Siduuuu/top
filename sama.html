Student from mount view scores the highest among all the students who appeared for class 12 examination 2079

Samikshya Adhikari, a student of mount view english boarding school got an outstanding result of 3.8 GPA in exams of class 12. She appeared for the examination this year and according to the reports she had already started to study in Gurukul CA aiming to become a CA.She is also said to have been the college topper in class 11 and the exams conducted in her school. So she was already brilliant in her studies. Wehope that she inspires more people to study hard, be determined and to follow the dreams and we also hope that she will perform brilliantly in her further studies and become a CA as soon as possible.
